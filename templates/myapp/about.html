Hello, I'm Dorice Obonyo

Welcome! I’m a blogger, developer, and lifelong learner from Nairobi, Kenya. Here, I share insights, experiences, and discoveries in technology, personal growth, and creativity.

My journey started with a curiosity about how things work and a drive to create meaningful solutions. Over time, I’ve been a software developer, technical writer, mentor, and student — each role shaping my perspective and deepening my passion for sharing knowledge.

When I’m not coding or writing, I’m exploring new technologies, reading great books, or enjoying Kenya’s vibrant culture and landscapes.
